Our Objective

The Odyssey Corporation is a community designed for Star Citizen. The Corporation is to create a community of like-minded pilots, players, enthusiasts, and the like to get together and enjoy the wonder that is Star Citizen with a mix of Milsim and Casual Playstyles. This Corporation is targeted to help new players get used to the game’s complexities and cater to more experienced players with player-held events and gatherings related to Star Citizen’s aspects. We want to emphasize having fun, growing together as a community, and ensuring everyone gets paid along the way. Your Star Citizen journey starts here; your Odyssey begins today.
